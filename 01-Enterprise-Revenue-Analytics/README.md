# 📊 Enterprise Revenue Pipeline & Executive Insights

![Enterprise Revenue Dashboard](Messy%20data%20dashboard.png)

## Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes enterprise sales performance by transforming raw transactional data into executive-level business insights. It enables decision-makers to monitor revenue, profitability, product performance, and regional sales trends through interactive visualizations.

---

## Business Objectives

- Monitor total revenue and profit
- Analyze monthly sales trends
- Compare product performance
- Evaluate regional sales distribution
- Support executive decision-making
